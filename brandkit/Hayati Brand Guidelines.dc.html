BRAND KIT v1.0 JULY 2026
01 — BRAND IDEA
The inside of a good marriage: warm, private, dignified, quietly playful.
The name
Hayati (حياتي / Hayatım) — "my life," the endearment spouses actually say to each other in both Arabic and Turkish. Never clinical, never neon-dating, never saccharine.
The motif
The pomegranate — symbol of love, abundance and marriage across Anatolian and Middle Eastern culture. Every mutual day adds a seed. Two seeds leaning together are the couple; the mark is the streak.
Working title — trademark and store-name search required before public launch. Vetted alternates: İkimiz · Baynana · Mawadda · Roohi.
