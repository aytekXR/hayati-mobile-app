Hayati is a private daily ritual for two.

Every day, you and your partner get one question — about your day, your memories, the small things and the big ones. You each answer on your own. Your partner's answer stays hidden until you've written yours, and then both are revealed together. No peeking, and no pressure to perform: just a moment that belongs to the two of you.

Answer together day after day and your shared streak grows, one pomegranate seed at a time. Miss a day? A weekly grace day keeps a good run alive. This is meant to feel gentle — never one more thing to feel guilty about.

Your daily question and your streak are always free.
